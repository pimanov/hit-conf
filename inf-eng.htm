OBJECTIVE

The aim of the Conference is to assemble active researchers in nonlinear hydrodynamics and students, and to serve as a forum where most recent developments can be discussed. It follows the tradition of biannual confer-ences organized by the Institute of Mechanics of Moscow State University between 1976 and 2004 (see http://hit2003.narod.ru/), which were widely attended by scientists and students from the former Soviet Union.

SCOPE

At this conference the following directions are to be considered:

  • Mathematical aspects of hydrodynamic instability;

  • Transition processes;

  • Chaos and turbulence;

  • Computational fluid dynamics;

  • Applications to microgravity sciences, geophysics, astrophysics, ecology etc.

In addition, in the framework of the Conference the Materials & Microgravity Sciences Workshop (MMSW) will be held for 2-3 days. A special workshop for students is also planned.

PROCEEDINGS

Abstracts of talks and posters will be published by Moscow University Press in "Proceedings of the Interna-tional Conference in Hydrodynamic Instability and Turbulence". Prospective participants must send abstracts in Word to the Organizing Committee (gertsens@imec.msu.ru) not later than 15 January 2006.

LANGUAGE

The official languages of the Conference are Russian and English.

LOCATION

The Conference will be held in a holiday hotel in the picturesque Moscow suburb. The average temperature of February in Moscow is about -8 grad C.
